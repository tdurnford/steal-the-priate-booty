# Code of Conduct

We expect everyone participating in this project to be respectful and professional.

## Be respectful
- No harassment or discriminatory language
- Assume good intent; ask clarifying questions
- Keep feedback focused on ideas and code, not people

## Be constructive
- Provide actionable feedback
- When you disagree, explain why and propose alternatives

Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation to maintain a safe, productive environment.
